Why should I join?

Gunn Quiz Bowl is a club that allows people to put their general knowledge to the test in a fast-paced
head to head competition with buzzers. Topics include history, literature, science, fine arts, current events, popular culture,
sports, and much more. Competitions this year will be held at local Bay Area Schools. Whether you want to test your
current knowledge of all sorts of topics, or just want to have fun and chill out, Gunn Quiz Bowl is the perfect club!
